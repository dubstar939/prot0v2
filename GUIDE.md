# 🎨 Prot0 Creative Suite: Your Ultimate User's Guide

Welcome to **Prot0 Creative Suite by 939Pro**! Whether you're a professional designer or just starting your creative journey, this suite is built to give you world-class editing power right in your browser.

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

1.  **Upload Your Vision**: Click the **"Upload Image"** button or simply drag and drop your photos anywhere on the workspace.
2.  **The Workspace**: 
    *   **Center**: Your main canvas.
    *   **Left Sidebar**: Your creative command center (Toolbox & Suite).
    *   **Right Sidebar**: Your Layer Stack (Manage folders, opacity, and blend modes).
    *   **Bottom Bar**: Contextual controls for whichever tool you're currently using.

---

## 🧰 The Toolbox (Core Editing)

These are your essential "bread and butter" tools for perfecting any image:

*   **✂️ Crop Tool**: Instantly optimize your composition with a smart center-square crop.
*   **🧽 Creative Eraser**: Paint over unwanted objects to remove them. 
    *   *Pro Tip*: Use the new **Undo/Redo** buttons (or `Ctrl+Z`) to refine your selection stroke-by-stroke!
*   **👤 BG Remove**: Isolate your subject with a single click. Perfect for creating stickers or product shots.
*   **🚀 Upscale & Enhance**: Give your low-res photos a professional "remastered" look.
*   **🧪 Creative Color**: Fine-tune brightness, saturation, and hue, or apply one of our curated **Looks Library** filters.
*   **🌫️ Creative Bokeh**: Add professional depth-of-field blur to your backgrounds.
*   **📷 RAW Developer**: Professional-grade control over Exposure, Temperature, Highlights, and Shadows.

## 📑 Mastering Layers

Prot0 uses a professional layer-based system:

*   **Visibility**: Toggle the 👁️ icon to hide/show layers.
*   **Opacity**: Slide the bar to create subtle overlays.
*   **Blend Modes**: Change how layers interact (Multiply, Screen, Overlay, etc.).
*   **Organization**: Click **"New Folder"** to group your assets and keep your workspace tidy.
*   **Merging**: Use **"Merge All"** to flatten your composition into a single high-quality layer.

## ⌨️ Pro Shortcuts

Work faster with these essential hotkeys:

| Shortcut | Action |
| :--- | :--- |
| `Ctrl + Z` | Undo (Global or Brush Stroke) |
| `Ctrl + Y` | Redo (Global or Brush Stroke) |
| `Shift + Ctrl + Z`| Redo (Alternative) |
| `[ ]` | (Coming Soon) Adjust Brush Size |

## 📲 Install as App

You can now save **Prot0 Creative Suite** directly to your home screen or desktop for a true full-screen, native experience:
1. Look for the **"Install App 📲"** button in the top header (it appears when your browser supports installation).
2. Follow the prompt to add it to your device.
3. Launch it anytime from your app drawer or home screen!

---

## 💾 Exporting Your Work

When your masterpiece is ready:
1. Click the **"Export"** button in the top right.
2. Choose your format (**JPEG**, **PNG**, or **WebP**).
3. Adjust the quality slider.
4. Hit **"Download"** or use the **"Share"** button to send it directly to your mobile device or social apps.
